INFORMATION FOR BOEING EMPLOYEES AND RETIREES

WORKLIFE RESOURCES FOR RETIREES EMPLOYEE/RETIREE CHANGE OF ADDRESS POWER OF ATTORNEY

Employee/Retiree Change Of Address

Current Boeing and subsidiary employee

Update home address in Worklife. Current Boeing and subsidiary employees unable to access Worklife online please call 1-866-473-2016 for assistance. Does not apply to subsidiaries of MAR, BDS, BI&A, Insitu and Liquid Robotics whose employees and former employees will coordinate updates according to their respective site business processes.

Retirees, former employees, beneficiaries, Qualified Domestic Relations Orders (QDROs) and individuals living outside the United States

Overview As a former employee, you may have a need to update your address on file. In addition to updating your address with The Boeing Company within Worklife, remember to update your address with the Benefit Suppliers referenced below to ensure that you receive relevant benefit-related communications in a timely manner. Former Boeing or subsidiary* employees who left the company since Jan. 1, 2011: If you are a former Boeing or subsidiary* employee, and have left the company on or after Jan. 1, 2011, you can update your address with The Boeing Company within Worklife. Terminated employees who are unable to access Worklife must contact EHD at (888) 469-0911 or (425) 234-0911.

Power of Attorneys (POA/Guardians/Conservators

NOTE: A Power of Attorney (POA)/Guardian/Conservator must have an approved POA/Guardianship/ Conservatorship document on file and approved by Boeing prior to submitting a change of address on behalf of the principal. If Boeing has a POA/Guardianship/Conservatorship on file and approved for the participant, and you are the approved agent: Make an address change by contacting Worklife at 1-866-473-2016 and say “Power of Attorney” when prompted. Active Employees may also update their address on Worklife at https://boeing.service-now.com/worklife.
